History

Founded in 2010, our company has been leading rafting expeditions in some of the most exciting rivers. Our experienced guides ensure safety and adventure on every trip.

Over the years, our rafting company has grown from a small local team into a trusted adventure destination. We expanded our routes, improved our equipment, and trained expert guides to ensure every guest feels safe and excited.
